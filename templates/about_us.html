About Tasty Truths

Tasty Truths is all about helping you find that perfect recipe hassle free. Our team consists of a group of hard working students from Drexel University, who were tired of trying to find recipes that were blocked behind paywalls or hundreds of different ads and wanted to make a change.


We designed Tasty Truths for a easy and interactive experience for our users. From easy to navigate menus to the ability to rate and leave a review, Tasty Truths has it all. On Tasty Truths, Users have the ability to express their creative side with recipes they have created or changed. People can than interact with these recipes to give feedback and suggestions or just give complements to the chef for their amazing creations.


If users are looking for more of a social involvement, Tasty Truths can help also. Our blog pages allows users to share their experiences in the kitchen for other users to see. You can ask for recommendations or for help if you are stuck and don't know why something turned out the way it did. Tasty Truths also has a contact page which can help you reach out to different chefs about questions or partnering up on a recipe.
